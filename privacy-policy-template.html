Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

MelonChat ("we", "our", or "us") is a customer service and messaging platform that helps businesses manage conversations with their customers across multiple channels, including Instagram Direct Messages.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with businesses using our platform via Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

When you send a message to a business through Instagram that uses our platform, we collect:

  • Profile Information: Your Instagram username, display name, and profile picture
  • Message Content: The text, images, videos, and other media you send
  • Metadata: Message timestamps and conversation history
  • Instagram User ID: Your unique Instagram identifier

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable businesses to receive and respond to your messages
  • Display your profile information to customer service representatives
  • Maintain conversation history for customer support purposes
  • Provide a unified inbox for businesses to manage customer communications
  • Improve our service quality and user experience

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without consent

We only share your information with:

  • The business you contacted: They can see your messages, name, and profile picture
  • Service providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase) and infrastructure partners who help us operate the platform
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure
  • We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data transmission
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We retain conversation data for as long as necessary to provide the service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out: Stop using our service by not messaging businesses that use our platform

7. Instagram Integration

Our platform integrates with Instagram via the Meta (Facebook) Instagram Messaging API. By sending messages to businesses using our platform, you acknowledge that:

  • Your messages are processed through Instagram's infrastructure
  • Instagram's own privacy policy also applies to your interactions
  • We access your Instagram profile information using Meta's Graph API

8. Data Retention

  • Active conversations: Retained while the business relationship is active
  • Closed conversations: Retained for up to 2 years for customer service purposes
  • Upon deletion request: Permanently deleted within 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.
